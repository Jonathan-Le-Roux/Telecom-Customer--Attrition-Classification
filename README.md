# Problem Statement

In today's highly competitive telecom industry, retaining users is of paramount importance. Subscriber attrition, whether to competitors or due to dissatisfaction, can greatly affect a company's performance and profitability. Your objective is to build a predictive model that, given the historical data of telecom customers, can identify new users who are at risk of attrition in the near future.

# Data Dictionary
All data is located [here](data/telecoms.csv)

<br>User ID: Unique identifier for each telecom user.
<br>**Mobile No**: User's mobile phone number.
<br>**User DOB**: Date of birth of the user.
<br>**User Job**: Occupation or job of the user.
<br>**Duration**: Total call duration (minutes) for the user.
<br>**Intl_Plan**: Whether the user has an international calling plan (Yes/No).
<br>**Voicemail_Plan**: Whether the user has a voicemail plan (Yes/No).
<br>**Voicemail_Messages**: Number of voicemail messages received by the user.
<br>**Day_Minutes**: Total minutes of daytime calls for the user.
<br>**Day_Calls**: Total number of daytime calls made by the user.
<br>**Day_Charge**: Total charge for daytime calls.
<br>**Eve_Minutes**: Total minutes of evening calls for the user.
<br>**Eve_Calls**: Total number of evening calls made by the user.
<br>**Eve_Charge**: Total charge for evening calls.
<br>**Night_Minutes**: Total minutes of nighttime calls for the user.
<br>**Night_Calls**: Total number of nighttime calls made by the user.
<br>**Night_Charge**: Total charge for nighttime calls.
<br>**Intl_Minutes**: Total minutes of international calls for the user.
<br>**Intl_Calls**: Total number of international calls made by the user.
<br>**Intl_Charge**: Total charge for international calls.
<br>**Service_Calls**: Number of user service calls made by the user.
<br>**Attrition**: The target variable indicates user attrition (Yes/No).
